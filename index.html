Travel.IO

How can we help users save money while planning and booking a trip.

My Role:

UX UI Designer

During my user interviews money proved to be a major factor to all the users in determining travel plans, saving for emergencies and or activities. Creating a budget for all the users needs would help the user make a more comfortable decision on planning a trip. If I can create a user friendly application to make travel plans and keep track of all travel expenses in one place, I believe it can help the users to remain organized and confident on a vacation or trip.

The Problem:

It's hard enough to plan a trip at a reasonable price not to mention keeping track of all that information.

The Solution:

What if you could have a user friendly application to make travel plans and keep track of all travel expenses in one place.

The Goal:

The goal was to design a user friendly application to help the user budget and save money while planning a trip and while traveling.

The Timeline:

Over the course of 6 weeks Travel.IO was designed to help users create a budget to plan a trip and activities before and while on the trip.
